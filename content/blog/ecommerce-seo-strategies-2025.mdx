# The Pulse of E-commerce: SEO Strategies for 2025

Search engine optimization is no longer just about keywords and backlinks. In 2025, the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence** and **User Intent** has created a new paradigm for online retailers.

## The AI Search Revolution

With the rise of Search Generative Experiences (SGE), users are getting direct answers to their queries. To survive, your e-commerce site must adapt:

### 1. **Semantic Authority**
Google now understands the *context* behind products. Instead of targeting "blue sneakers," aim for "lightweight breathable sneakers for marathon running."

### 2. **Visual & Voice Search Synergy**
Optimizing your product images with high-quality metadata and descriptive alt-text is critical as more users search via camera or voice commands.
